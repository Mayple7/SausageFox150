Mansion Mashers (Action, Adventure, Hack and Slash)

Mansion Mashers is a fast paced, side scrolling game where the main character is a princess from the Fox Clan. The Dog Clan are the antagonists and they have just launched an attack on your mansion! While defending your home from the invaders, you have learned that Prince Oakley has been kidnapped! Fight through numerous levels littered with enemies, and encounter frightening bosses so you can save your prince and Mash their Mansion!
